VAD++: Learning Trajectory Offsets for

Improved End-to-End Autonomous Driving

Zhennan Wang 1, Jianing Teng 1, Xing Pan1,
1HAOMO.AI Technology Co., Ltd
arXiv Code

Abstract

End-to-end autonomous driving has made encouraging progress recently, such as VAD. In this technical report, we utilize the VAD framework to implement several enhancements, particularly by transitioning from directly learning trajectories to learning trajectory offsets. Our improved model, VAD++, dramatically decreases the average planning displacement error by 39.7% (0.78m vs. 0.47m) and the average collision rate by 84.2% (0.38% vs. 0.06%) over the model of VAD paper. In addition, the VAD++ planner is capable of achieving diverse multi- mode trajectories.
